Everything You Need to Run a Clean Server

From AI-powered threat detection to full moderation tooling, Sentinel replaces a stack of plugins with one intelligent suite.

5 AI Agents

Triage, Ban Draft, Natural-Language Search, Rule Lookup, and Anti-Cheat agents work together to surface threats and draft responses.

Player Management

Kick, ban, warn, mute, and freeze with full audit trails. Spectate and teleport with terrain-aware safety checks.

In-Game CUI Panel

A rich admin panel rendered inside Rust's native UI. Seven views, hotkey binding, and zero FPS impact when closed.

Web Panel

Manage your server from any browser or phone. Token-authenticated, mobile-first, and polling live data from your server.

Discord Integration

Rich embeds for every action. Route bans, kicks, AI alerts, and daily digests to the right channels automatically.

Permission Groups

Role-based access with Oxide/Carbon integration. Admin, Moderator, and Trial Mod defaults out of the box.

Ready in Under Two Minutes

No external databases, no complex setup. Drop in the plugin, paste your config, and go.

1

Install

Drop Sentinel.cs into your Oxide or Carbon plugins folder. The database auto-creates on first start.

2

Configure

Set your web panel token, Discord webhooks, and optional BYOK LLM keys via the onboarding wizard.

3

Protect

AI agents begin monitoring immediately. Review triage flags, approve ban drafts, and keep your server clean.
